Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Privacy with Snopy

Introduction

Welcome to Snopy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide you with our services:

  • Shop Owners: Name, email, address, phone number, shop name, and other shop-related details.
  • Users: Name, email, and address.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Enable account creation, login, and logout.
  • Allow users to search for shops, rate them, follow or unfollow shops.
  • Provide basic app functionalities (e.g., displaying profiles).

Features Coming Soon

The following features are under development and will be available in future updates:

  • Messaging between users and shops.
  • Posting and sharing content.
  • Deleting or editing profiles.

Currently, these features are displayed in the app but are not functional.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any company or third party.

Your Rights

As a user, you can:

  • Create an account.
  • Log in and log out of the app.
  • Search for shops and rate or follow them.

In the future, you will also be able to delete your account and profile information.

Monetization and Advertisements

Snopy does not use advertisements or any monetization. The app is purely a service to connect users with shops.
